About Training Centre Management System (TCMS)

Training Centre Management System (TCMS) is a web-based software to aid Training Centre Administrative Management Needs. The TCMS is capable of managing Enquiry Details, Payment Details, Student Details, Faculty Details etc. with high efficiency and easy and user-friendly manner.It also provides detailed and accurate report for managing the Training Centre

The system should be able to manage the training activities being organized at the centre. The System should provide role-based access to various category of users in the training institute like the Head of the Institution, Head of various department, Faculty of different programmes, admin staff and its student. The system should be able to take care of the complete workflow from the time a student registers to the time he/she passes out and is awarded a certificate. The Training Management System should be user friendly, secure, robust and scalable. It should also support the integration with social media (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram etc.). The institute can have a page on these social media platforms and the training Management System should have facility for publishing their events and interesting content related to the institute on these pages.
